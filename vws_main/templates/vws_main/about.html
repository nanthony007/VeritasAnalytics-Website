About Me

I have been wrestling for 20 of my 24 years on this planet and can say without a doubt it is the single most important, influential force in my life. Wrestling and wrestlers have taught me everything I know from values to work ethic from humility to pride and from confidence to despair.

My pursuits through wrestling have guided me to degrees in Learning and Cognition and Computer Science. I hope to combine these fields and apply them to Perfomance Analysis (a type of Data Science). This is a niche field for studying human performance over time and, for me, wrestling seems like the obvious place to start.

I started my wrestling career at the age of 4 in central Pennsylvania. Continuing through middle school and high school in the Blue Mountain School District I enjoyed average success. My sophomore year my family moved to Florida where I finished my high school career in three years instead of four and continued wrestling for the last year. After graduating high school I attended the University of Florida where I wrestled in the NCWA for two years before transitioning to a coaching role. A quick tanget... this site currently supports only Freestyle wrestling so you will not find any collegiate or high school results here. This is intentional and has nothing to do with capability but was simply a matter of personal preference... Freestyle > Folkstyle.

My goals for this site are to provide some extra context for international Freestyle wrestling and wrestling in general. I want to give the dedicated fan a place to really dig in and follow their favorite wrestlers and get in depth analysis of their matches and events. I want to provide a launching ground so that one day wrestling will have the advanced analytics that other sports have.
Key features of the site include:

  1. User login interface to easily track your favorite wrestlers.
  2. Report Feed to keep track of analysis documents for each event.
  3. Elo Rating system (adapted formula from chess).
  4. Wrestler profile pages to see your wrestlers stats at a glance.
  5. (pending...) Dashboard to view your "team" stats for each user.
    • This could be expanded into a competitve system in the future.
