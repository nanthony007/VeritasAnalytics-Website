Welcome


Veritas Analytics is a dedicated open-source statistics provider for the sport of wrestling.
You can find all the content for this website and the GUI collector program on GitHub here.
There is a file with descriptions and formulas in the repository for your reference.

Built using Python, Django, and Postgres | Powered by Heroku
Developed and maintained by Nicholas Anthony


Developer's Corner


Wrestling is my passion. It overflows into every other aspect of my life.

It is interesting how wrestling is the oldest sport, yet it has struggled to emerge into the 21st century. Almost having been removed from the 2020 Olympic Games was a disaster and has prompted me to make efforts to marketize the sport myself. This site is dedicated to educating the new and experienced fan, and performance measurement for wrestler/coach evaluations, and to increase the general fan-base by providing a unified fantasy system.
-Nick
